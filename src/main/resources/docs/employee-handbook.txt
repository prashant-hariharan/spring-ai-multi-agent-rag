Employee Handbook (Internal)

Work Hours:
- Standard business hours are 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM local time.
- Flexible scheduling may be approved by reporting managers.

Leave Policy:
- Paid time off (PTO) is accrued monthly.
- Sick leave can be availed as per HR guidelines.

Code of Conduct:
- Maintain professional behavior in all communication channels.
- Follow company data security and confidentiality policies.

Remote Work:
- Employees must follow approved remote work guidelines.
- Security controls (VPN, MFA) are mandatory for remote access.
